# GPT Additions — Potential Weapons and Tools

Date: 2026-06-16
Label: Ziptide weapon/tool direction

## Design direction

Ziptide should avoid feeling like a normal shooter. The player's gear should feel like explorer tech: non-lethal, modular, physical, readable in VR, and useful for both combat and puzzles.

Core rule: no bullets as the default fantasy. Prefer electricity, gravity, bubbles, light, sound, foam, drones, shields, scanning, and strange alien tools.

Creatures should generally not drop weapons. They should drop materials, samples, crystals, charge organs, shell fragments, or Bloom residue that can be used to craft or upgrade tech.

## Unique Ziptide-style gear

### Taser / Stun Dart Tool

Family-friendly starter weapon.

Possible variants:

- Single dart: stuns a small drone or creature.
- Chain dart: arcs to a nearby second target.
- Anchor dart: pins a target to a surface briefly.
- Pulse dart: creates a small stun burst on impact.
- Tag dart: marks target for RILL, drones, or tracking.
- Overcharge dart: hold trigger for a stronger stun.

### Bubble Snare Launcher

Shoots a floating bubble that traps or slows enemies.

Uses:

- Fully traps small enemies.
- Slows medium enemies.
- Makes flying enemies float off-course.
- Can be popped for a small knockback burst.
- Can temporarily hold objects in puzzle spaces.

### Gravity Glove

A hand-based tool that pulls, pushes, suspends, or throws objects.

Uses:

- Pull small objects toward the player.
- Hold stunned drones in midair.
- Throw stunned enemies or debris.
- Place batteries into sockets.
- Push objects/enemies away with palm gesture.

Recommended loop: stun dart first, then gravity glove can grab stunned drones.

### Prism Beam

A continuous light tool that can also be a weapon.

Uses:

- Stun or weaken light-sensitive creatures.
- Reflect off prism surfaces.
- Split through crystals.
- Burn away Bloom growth.
- Activate light-based puzzle locks.

### Sonic Thumper

Short-range sound pulse.

Uses:

- Push enemies back.
- Break fragile crystals.
- Reveal hidden tunnels.
- Disorient flying enemies.
- Clear swarms without precise aim.

### Foam Cannon

Shoots expanding hard-light foam.

Uses:

- Stick enemies to the floor.
- Temporarily block tunnels.
- Patch ship/base leaks.
- Create small platforms.
- Slow moving hazards or conveyor objects.

### Magnet Lasso

A glowing tether tool.

Uses:

- Pull metal objects.
- Trip mechanical enemies.
- Attach objects together.
- Yank shields off drones.
- Swing or pull cargo in later traversal systems.

### Static Net

Throwable electric net/grid.

Uses:

- Catch flying enemies.
- Reveal invisible creatures.
- Electrify a doorway.
- Create temporary crowd-control barriers.

### Bloom Splicer

Alien-biotech ecosystem tool.

Uses:

- Rewrite hostile vines into bridges.
- Convert nests into resource pods.
- Open organic doors.
- Temporarily turn hostile plants into defense systems.

### Orbital Pebble / RILL Orb

A small floating assistant orb.

Modes:

- Shield orbit.
- Light source.
- Scan helper.
- Zap nearest small threat.
- Decoy projection.
- Fetch tiny objects.

## Classic sci-fi gear, adapted for Ziptide

### Stun Blaster

One-handed energy tool. Slow glowing bolts, stun status, charge-shot knockback.

### Plasma Cutter

Short-range beam that cuts vines, cables, doors, and crystals. More tool than weapon.

### Arc Rifle

Two-handed electric beam. Chains through swarms and can power machines.

### Pulse Cannon

Large charged shot. Pushes enemies, opens heavy doors, breaks shields. High cooldown.

### Cryo Sprayer

Freezes or slows enemies, stops moving machines, makes temporary ice surfaces.

### Heat Lance

Melts ice, burns vines, activates thermal switches, overheats armor.

### Drone Launcher

Deploys small helper drones: zap drone, shield drone, repair drone, scan drone, lure drone.

### Energy Shield Disc

Thrown shield/boomerang. Blocks projectiles, ricochets, returns to hand, activates distant switches.

## Movement and belt gadgets

### Drift Belt

Short low-gravity glide. Tap to drift forward, hold to slow-fall, flick to air-dash. Keep comfort limits strict.

### Bubble Lift Capsule

Throwable bubble that lifts the player or an object upward.

### Gravity Flip Charm

Brief wall/ceiling gravity effect. Strong VR comfort risk, so use only in controlled/snap zones.

### Hover Shell

Low-altitude hover pad under the player. Crosses acid/water/lava without full jetpack flight.

### Zipline Seed

Creates temporary energy line between two anchors. Useful for traversal and moving cargo.

### Blink Buoy

Throw a marker and teleport to it after a delay. Short range and clear line-of-sight only.

### Pocket Platform

Drops a small floating platform. Can be traversal, cover, or puzzle object.

### Phase Umbrella

Handheld/belt-deployed slow-fall shield. Can catch wind currents and block hazards from above.

## Signature Ziptide ideas

### Tide Glove

Creates wave-like force in air. Pushes enemies, moves floating objects, clears fog, redirects projectiles.

### Echo Seed

Repeats the player's last action. Could hold pressure plates, repeat a stun pulse, distract enemies, or mimic a gesture.

### RILL Projector

Turns the companion into a tactical tool: decoy, flashlight, scan pulse, shield bubble, lure voice.

### Mini Gate Puck

Creates a small temporary object portal. Start with small objects only; full player portals are much harder.

### Bloom Tamer

Temporarily controls a small creature. Later feature, not starter tech.

## Implementation difficulty guide

Easy:

- Simple stun projectile.
- Scan pulse.
- Simple shield bubble.
- Basic cooldown items.
- Simple beam.
- Throwable pulse grenade.

Medium:

- Chain lightning.
- Bubble trap.
- Gravity push/pull.
- Deployable drone.
- Hover belt.
- Reflecting prism beam.
- Pocket platform.

Hard:

- Wall walking.
- Portals.
- Swing/tether physics.
- Creature taming.
- Multi-phase cinematic powers.
- Anything that rapidly moves the VR player.

Guiding rule: start with tools that move objects and affect enemies before tools that move the player.
