## Short summary

The brief frames the first world as a compact onboarding planet, not a giant open-world city. It asks Architect to graybox a playable first-world outline with:

- Starter Hub / Dock Quarter
- Spaceport
- Ground Vehicle Port
- Toxic City Main Spine
- Sludge Canals
- Slum Walkways
- Outskirts Transition
- Open Badlands Vehicle Area
- Badlands Mission Pocket
- Dormant Ziptide Site

The player should quickly get a taste of movement, exploration, basic tools/weapons, a short spaceport mission, a short badlands/vehicle mission, and then the introduction to the Ziptide portal/gate concept.

## Key instruction

Do not overbuild this first world. It is an onboarding world that should get the player to the broader other-planets premise quickly. Prioritize graybox scale, pathing, region hierarchy, landmarks, future vehicle space, and mission placeholders over final visuals.
