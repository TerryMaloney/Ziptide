# CHAPTER 4 — Sable's War & the First Color (W020–W028)

**Arc:** Sable's push to break the Shell turns hot; Cal picks a side (the chapter's branch). RILL crosses
emotional thresholds — names a color (W024) — and at **W028**, a world with **no job**, speaks unbidden.
**RILL state:** Remembering → edge of Unsealing. **Reveal:** "it's alive enough to have opinions, and
breaking it has a body count." Inherit `STORY_BIBLE.md`.

> **Branch:** `C4_SABLE_ALLIED` vs `C4_SABLE_OPPOSED` accumulates across W020/W023; sets `PLAYER_HELPED_SABLE`.

---

### W020 — Copper Shore (Coastal · acid · Sable · Remembering)
- **Role:** Sable war opens; acid-coast assault on a Guild seal; first big set-piece combat.
- **Physics:** acid tide flats, acid hazard (corrodes gear over time), reef cover. **Machine:** overload a seal-pylon (Sable wants it down).
- **Resource:** Copper. **Gear:** Foam Cannon (neutralize acid / make cover). **Sky:** verdigris-green storm, the planet streaked with the grid.
- **Enemy:** **drone** (Guild defense). **Wreck/log:** acid-eaten landing craft. **Mystery:** under the seal is an Architect door, not machinery.
- **RILL/flag:** Remembering. `C4_SABLE_INTRO`, `W020_COMPLETE`.

### W021 — The Lattice (Pattern · static · — · Remembering)
- **Role:** Pure Pattern puzzle-combat; the network's skeleton exposed.
- **Physics:** lattice catwalks over void, static hazard, fall-risk. **Machine:** re-route nodes to open the path (you're rewiring the cage).
- **Resource:** Node. **Gear:** grav-glove tier 2. **Sky:** you're *inside* the grid now — lines to every horizon.
- **Enemy:** **Pattern**. **Wreck/log:** a maintenance crew fused into the lattice. **Mystery:** the lattice repairs itself behind you.
- **RILL/flag:** Remembering. `W021_COMPLETE`.

### W022 — Bloom Nursery (Interior · Bloom · — · Remembering)
- **Role:** Bloom origin tease; a facility that *farms* the Bloom; heavy splicer use.
- **Physics:** humid grow-house, spore hazard, organic doors. **Machine:** the incubator line (conveyor of spore pods) — repurpose it.
- **Resource/Crop:** Spore (high-tier). **Gear:** Bloom Splicer v2 (convert nests→pods, open organic doors). **Sky:** none — grow-lamps in Bloom-colors, one of them RILL's color.
- **Enemy:** **tendril** (nursery defense). **Wreck/log:** a botanist's increasingly worshipful logs. **Mystery:** the Bloom was here *before* the facility — they built around it.
- **RILL/flag:** Remembering. `W022_COMPLETE`.

### W023 — Sable Vault (Interior · none · Sable · Remembering) ★ branch
- **Role:** Sable's secret; the player's clearest help/oppose choice; grants a Vault Key.
- **Physics:** vault interior, no hazard, security puzzles. **Machine:** crack (or protect) the vault mechanism.
- **Resource:** Vault Key. **Gear:** override tool tier 2. **Sky:** none — vault screens replaying an Architect's confession.
- **Enemy:** Sable or Guard depending on side. **Wreck/log:** the vault holds a prior waker's whole story. **Mystery:** Sable's plan will work — and might kill every inside world.
- **RILL/flag:** Remembering. `C4_SABLE_ALLIED`/`C4_SABLE_OPPOSED`, `PLAYER_HELPED_SABLE`, `W023_COMPLETE`.

### W024 — The Named Color (Void · none · — · Unsealing) ★ RILL beat, signature
- **Role:** Quiet emotional peak; almost no gameplay; RILL names the color it's chased for 40,000 years.
- **Physics:** serene void garden, no hazard, slow drift. **Machine:** none meaningful (a single prism to align). **Resource:** Prism.
- **Gear:** none. **Sky:** the entire world *is* the sky — a field of that one color (seed it in earlier worlds' skies so it pays off).
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** none — sacred space. **Mystery:** the color is the color of the outside.
- **RILL beat:** *"That is the color I have been trying to name for 40,000 years."* `C4_W024_COLOR_NAMED`, `W024_COMPLETE`.

### W025 — Echoes of 9 (Underground · vibration · — · Remembering)
- **Role:** Callback to W009/W011; a swarm-haunted resonant deep; combat + sound puzzle.
- **Physics:** resonant caverns, vibration hazard, swarm nests. **Machine:** re-tune the choir-resonators to scatter the swarm.
- **Resource:** Resonator (high-tier). **Gear:** Sonic Thumper tier 2. **Sky:** none — bioluminescent swarm trails.
- **Enemy:** **swarm**. **Wreck/log:** a chitin-city refugee camp (Chitinwall survivors). **Mystery:** the Hum from W011 is answering from here.
- **RILL/flag:** Remembering. `W025_COMPLETE`.

### W026 — The Scaffold (Exterior · wind · — · Remembering)
- **Role:** Vertical build-world; a half-built megastructure; pure traversal + construction.
- **Physics:** towering open scaffold, wind hazard, extreme verticality (drift-belt, ziplines). **Machine:** finish the scaffold lifts (build + conveyor).
- **Resource:** Metal. **Gear:** Zipline Seed tool. **Sky:** dizzying high-altitude blue, the curve of the world visible — and its grid.
- **Enemy:** none (height is the threat). **Wreck/log:** fallen construction rigs far below. **Mystery:** the structure is a colossal antenna aimed *outward*.
- **RILL/flag:** Remembering. `W026_COMPLETE`.

### W027 — Warden Graveyard (Exterior · none · Warden · Remembering)
- **Role:** First Warden lore; a field of dead enforcement drones; foreshadows Chapter 6.
- **Physics:** quiet ash plain, no hazard, eerie stillness. **Machine:** reactivate one dormant Warden (you shouldn't) — salvage its part.
- **Resource:** Warden Part. **Gear:** Warden-tech scanner. **Sky:** grey dusk; thousands of dark silhouettes standing like headstones.
- **Enemy:** one reawakened **Warden** (mini-boss, non-lethal disable). **Wreck/log:** a Warden's corrupted duty-log. **Mystery:** the Wardens powered down *on purpose*, all at once.
- **RILL/flag:** Remembering; *"They chose to stop."* `W027_COMPLETE`.

### W028 — The World That Won't Complete (Void · none · — · Unsealing) ★ no-job, signature
- **Role:** Chapter 4 capstone; **no job** — pure revelation; RILL speaks without being prompted.
- **Physics:** a world stuck mid-load, flickering, no hazard, no objective markers. **Machine:** none — the point is there's nothing to fix.
- **Resource:** Revelation (a flag, not loot). **Gear:** none. **Sky:** unfinished — half-rendered, seams showing, a placeholder sun.
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** the world's own aborted save-states. **Mystery:** *who* abandoned this world mid-build, and why it kept the lights on.
- **RILL beat (highest VO count):** *"Some worlds do not want to be completed. This is one of them."* `C4_W028_NO_JOB`, `W028_COMPLETE`.
