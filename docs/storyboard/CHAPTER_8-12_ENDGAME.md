# CHAPTERS 8–12 — Revelation, Branch & The Four Endings (W062–W068)

**Arc:** W062 delivers the revelation (the Shell kept something *out*; the lab). W063 is **the Branching** —
the player's accumulated choices set the ending. W064–W067 are the four **approach** worlds (one per
ending), and **W068** is the convergence where each resolves. **RILL state:** Integrated → one of four
Endgame states. **Reveal:** total. Inherit `STORY_BIBLE.md` §8 (endings). Highest VO budget in the game.

> Branch math: W063 reads the `PLAYER_*` flags (Mara/Sable/Warden, repaired/destroyed seal, trusted/
> ignored RILL) + the W063 choice → sets `C12_W063_ENDING_A/B/C/D` → routes to the matching approach world.

---

### W062 — The Revelation Chamber (Interior · none · — · Integrated) ★ RILL beat, signature
- **Role:** The recontextualization. RILL delivers the core line; everything reframes.
- **Physics:** the Architects' revelation chamber, no hazard, awe. **Machine:** the Truth Engine — assembling the Signal Core here *shows* the outside.
- **Resource:** Revelation. **Gear:** none. **Sky:** the chamber dome dissolves to show the lab beyond the glass — the Observers at work.
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** the Architects' explanation of *why* the Shell is two-way. **Mystery:** the thing kept out has been *knocking*.
- **RILL beat (max VO):** *"The Architects did not build this to keep stories in. They built it to keep something out."* `C8_W062_REVELATION`, `W062_COMPLETE`.

### ★ EARTH APPROACH — between W062 and W063 (Terry's idea; PROPOSED, see `STORY_BIBLE.md` §8b)
The climax staging that turns the abstract "outside" physical. Slots after the Revelation, before the
Branch (no table renumber — it's the *setting* the endgame worlds use):
- **The Lagrange door.** W062's coordinates point to **Jupiter's L4/L5 Lagrange point** in the observers'
  real universe — the Shell's only true door. (Real orbital mechanics — the nerd payoff.)
- **The cloaked ship.** A hidden vessel waits there (Architects'/prior-wakers'); boarding it = Cal's first
  unambiguous step *outside the cage*. Ties to the ship north-star.
- **Earth level (the task).** Fly in-system to **Earth** — the Observers' world, where the lab is. A
  grounded, all-Earth mission (reach/infiltrate the lab; one concrete objective). First real-world
  environment after 60+ alien worlds = the gut-punch that earns the ending. *(Distinct world; Earth art
  kit — design now, art later. Use a late slot or fold into the W064–W067 approach worlds as Earth-set.)*
- → leads directly into **W063 The Branching**, now staged on/at Earth.

### W063 — The Branching (Void/Earth · none · — · Integrated→Endgame) ★ the choice
- **Role:** The single biggest player choice; the four endings diverge here.
- **Physics:** a crossroads in the void, four gates, no hazard. **Machine:** the gate-key cradle — *what Cal does with the Signal Core + RILL* decides everything.
- **Resource:** Choice (flag). **Gear:** none. **Sky:** four different skies behind four gates (Reseal / Cross / Forget / Become).
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** every prior waker's choice, displayed — none chose the same. **Mystery:** there's a fifth gate, dark (sequel/DLC hook).
- **RILL/flag:** Integrated; defers to Cal. `C12_W063_BRANCH` → `C12_W063_ENDING_A/B/C/D`, `W063_COMPLETE`.

### W064 — Ending A Approach · Reseal / Steward (Void · none · — · Endgame A)
- **Role:** Approach for ending **A** — repair the Shell, stay as caretaker; the worlds live on, asleep, safe.
- **Physics:** the network being made whole, no hazard, restorative. **Machine:** the final reseal (your last, biggest repair).
- **Resource:** Signal. **Sky:** the grid healing into something almost beautiful; the crack closing.
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** wakers who also chose to stay. **Mystery:** does sealing it keep the outside thing out — or trap Cal forever, willingly?
- **RILL/flag:** Endgame A (RILL stays). 12–20 VO lines. `C12_W063_ENDING_A`.

### W065 — Ending B Approach · Cross / Break Out (Void · none · — · Endgame B)
- **Role:** Approach for ending **B** — open the door and leave with RILL into the outside/lab.
- **Physics:** the gate opening to the outside, void hazard at the threshold. **Machine:** force the gate (the riskiest "repair-in-reverse").
- **Resource:** Signal. **Sky:** the Shell parts; the lab's impossible daylight pours in.
- **Enemy:** none (the threshold itself). **Wreck/log:** the one waker who got the door open and never came back. **Mystery:** does the inside survive being unobserved?
- **RILL/flag:** Endgame B (RILL crosses with Cal). `C12_W063_ENDING_B`.

### W066 — Ending C Approach · Forget / Reset (Void · none · — · Endgame C)
- **Role:** Approach for ending **C** — let it reset; RILL chooses to forget so the worlds stay innocent.
- **Physics:** a gentle unwinding, no hazard, elegiac. **Machine:** the reset cradle (undo the waking).
- **Resource:** Signal. **Sky:** every world's sky cycling back to its first dawn.
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** the loop's prior iterations — this has happened before. **Mystery:** will Cal remember? Will any of it have mattered?
- **RILL/flag:** Endgame C (RILL forgets, mercy). `C12_W063_ENDING_C`.

### W067 — Ending D Approach · Become / Pattern (Void · pattern · — · Endgame D)
- **Role:** Approach for ending **D** — merge with the Pattern; the universe authors itself.
- **Physics:** dissolving into Pattern, pattern hazard everywhere, transcendent traversal. **Machine:** the merge engine (give yourself to it).
- **Resource:** Signal. **Sky:** all skies becoming one self-writing Pattern in the named color.
- **Enemy:** **Pattern** (welcoming, not hostile). **Wreck/log:** the Broken Pattern (W054) was the warning; this is the right way. **Mystery:** is becoming everything the same as dying?
- **RILL/flag:** Endgame D (RILL becomes the Pattern). `C12_W063_ENDING_D`.

### W068 — The Final World (Void · all · — · Endgame X) ★ convergence
- **Role:** All four endings converge and resolve here; the credits-image of each is unique.
- **Physics:** the universe's last moment, all hazards present-but-transcended. **Machine:** the final act (varies by ending). **Resource:** Resolution.
- **Gear:** none. **Sky:** the definitive shot of each ending (steward / outside / reset / Pattern).
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** Cal's own marker, planted for the next waker — the loop, or its end.
- **RILL/flag:** the four-variant ending VO. `C12_W068_COMPLETE`.

---
**Endgame design notes:** keep each approach world short and *felt*, not grindy. The four endings are
**equal** — no "true" ending in the base game; the dark fifth gate at W063 reserves room for DLC. Earn the
ending through the whole journey's flags, not a last-second menu pick.
