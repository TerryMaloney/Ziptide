# CHAPTER 6 — The Wardens & RILL's Name (W039–W051)

**Arc:** The longest chapter — the Warden arc. The Pattern warns it spreads toward memory; the Wardens
(the Shell's immune system) become allies or enemies by Cal's choices; Mara makes a final offer (W046);
the Architects' Chamber (W047) reveals who they worked for; and at **W051**, RILL **names itself**. **RILL
state:** Unsealing → naming. **Reveal:** "the cage has a conscience too, and so does RILL." Inherit
`STORY_BIBLE.md`.

> **Branch:** `C6_WARDEN_ALLY` vs `C6_WARDEN_ENEMY` (built across W037/W043/W049); `PLAYER_HELPED_WARDEN`.
