# CHAPTERS 0–1 — Arrival & First Doubt (W000–W004)

**Arc:** Cal arrives in the Ziptide network and takes her first contracts. A routine repair behaves
*wrong*; the Bloom makes first contact; RILL boots. By the end she suspects the network isn't what she
was told. **RILL state:** Dormant. **Signal:** 0→Threshold 1. **Reveal level:** "it's a job" → "this
repair behaves wrong." Inherit meta from `STORY_BIBLE.md`. Seeds below; deepen each into a per-world
README via `_WORLD_TEMPLATE.md` when we build it (W001–W004 get full READMEs first).

> Seed format: **Wxxx — Name** (Type · hazard · faction · RILL). identity/role · physics · machine ·
> crop/resource · gear · sky · enemy · wreck+log · mystery object · RILL beat/flag.

### W000 — The Drift In (Tutorial · none · — · Dormant)
- **Role:** Chapter 0 onboarding aboard Cal's own ship as it slots into the network; learn move/grab/holster/travel.
- **Physics:** ship-interior, stable g. **Machine:** prime the ship's gate-coupler (build/repair tutorial).
- **Resource:** none. **Gear:** start kit (taser). **Sky:** the Ziptide gate ring blooming open ahead — first awe shot.
- **Enemy:** none. **Wreck/log:** your own ship's prior-owner log (tone-setter). **Mystery:** the coupler reads a frequency that "shouldn't have a sender."
- **RILL/flag:** RILL dark (pre-boot). `FIRST_TRAVEL`, `TUTORIAL_COMPLETE`.

### W001 — Toxic Venice (City · Bloom · Wake Guild · Dormant) ★ signature, full README exists
- **Role:** First contract; RILL boots; first Bloom; the relay comes up *wrong*. (See `W001_ToxicCity/`.)
- **Physics:** layered canal city, toxic sludge below (no-stand hazard). **Machine:** re-seat the signal-relay node (it's a *seal*, not a relay).
- **Resource:** Signal Node. **Gear:** taser + wrist scanner. **Sky:** overcast industrial haze, oligarch towers, a dim banded planet low on the horizon.
- **Enemy:** feral maintenance **drones**. **Wreck/log:** a berthed scavenger hull; log: *"third time the dock's eaten my deposit."* **Mystery:** the relay's signature "doesn't match local tech."
- **RILL beat:** boot — *"Systems nominal. I think."* `C1_W001_RILL_BOOT`, `W001_COMPLETE`.

### W002 — The Dry Cistern (Underground · none · — · Dormant)
- **Role:** First descent; teaches mining + dark traversal; a swarm guards the deep water-works.
- **Physics:** pitch-dark caverns, echo, no hazard but verticality. **Machine:** restart the cistern pumps (conveyor of ore buckets).
- **Resource:** Mineral (mine nodes). **Gear:** headlamp/scan upgrade. **Sky:** none (underground) — a single shaft of surface light as a landmark.
- **Enemy:** **swarm** (small skittering Bloom-bugs). **Wreck/log:** a flooded digger; log: *"the pumps were never for water."* **Mystery:** the cistern is far older than the city above it — Architect stonework.
- **RILL/flag:** Dormant functional lines. `W002_COMPLETE`.

### W003 — Glass Shelf (Exterior · wind · — · Dormant)
- **Role:** First open-sky world; wind as a physics hazard; introduces tendrils.
- **Physics:** high windswept glass mesas, gusts shove you (comfort-safe nudge), fall-risk ledges. **Machine:** raise wind-baffle relays.
- **Resource:** Crystal (harvest from glass blooms). **Gear:** magnet-tether/anchor tool. **Sky:** vast and clear — *two* moons and a faint geometric shimmer at the zenith (first subliminal Pattern seed).
- **Enemy:** **tendril** (wind-whipped Bloom vines). **Wreck/log:** a crashed glider-skiff; log: *"the wind always pushes you back toward the gate."* **Mystery:** the crystals ring at a frequency matching W001's relay.
- **RILL/flag:** Dormant. `W003_COMPLETE`.

### W004 — The Broadcast Tomb (Interior · static · — · Dormant→stirs) ★ RILL beat
- **Role:** Chapter 1 capstone; a dead broadcast station; RILL asks its first real question.
- **Physics:** sealed interior, static discharge hazard (zaps gear), dead silence. **Machine:** restore the broadcast spine (you're amplifying the *containment* signal without knowing it).
- **Resource:** Memory Shard. **Gear:** signal-dampener mod. **Sky:** none — but a wall of dead screens, one flickering an image of a sky that isn't here.
- **Enemy:** none — dread, not combat. **Wreck/log:** the station *is* the wreck; logs are the staff's last broadcast. **Mystery:** the final broadcast is addressed to "the watchers."
- **RILL beat:** *"What are you carrying that requires containment?"* `C1_BLOOM_FIRST_CONTACT` (earlier), `C1_WAKE_GUILD_INTRO`, `W004_COMPLETE`, `SIGNAL_THRESHOLD_1`.
